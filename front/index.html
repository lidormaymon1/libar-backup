Welcome to Liaberean Staff Web Portal!

Efficiency and convenience at your fingertips. Our dedicated web platform is designed exclusively for Liaberean staff members to streamline your daily operations and enhance your library management experience. With a range of powerful features, you can easily handle book inventory, customer management, and loan administration.

Key Features:

  • Book Management: Add, update, and delete books effortlessly. Maintain accurate records of our extensive collection to ensure easy access and organization.
  • Loan Administration: Track loaned books and manage due dates efficiently. Receive alerts for expired loans and facilitate hassle-free returns.
  • Customer Management: Easily manage customer information, including adding new customers and removing outdated records.
